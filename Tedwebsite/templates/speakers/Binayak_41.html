Binayak Acharya


Binayak Acharya is the Founder and CEO of ThinkZone, a venture-backed and World Bank-award winning social organization that aims to improve the learning outcome of children in low-resource settings. With the efficacious combination of instructor-led support and easily available digital solutions, ThinkZone empowers educators and parents, to ensure that children have access to high-quality learning experiences. It intends to reduce the learning gaps faced by students from underserved neighborhoods.
