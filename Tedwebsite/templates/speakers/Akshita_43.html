Akshita Jain


A 21-year-old B.Tech student at IIT Guwahati, Akshita Jain has been a nature and wildlife lover since childhood. She is a very passionate wildlife and travel photographer and is also a classical dancer. Her photography work has received several national and international laurels and is published by esteemed organizations like National Geographic India. In her journey as a wildlife photographer, Akshita witnessed some breath-taking moments in the forests and got new perspectives about nature and the environment. She strongly believes that love for nature and wildlife among humans would bring about real change when it comes to saving the depleting environment and forests. She uses her pictures to show the beauty of wildlife and nature, for people to fall in love with the wild.
